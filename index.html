What is Elevate Volleyball

Elevate Volleyball is built for players who want more than just court time — a consistent place to compete, grow, and belong. We keep the standard high, the environment welcoming, and the community at the center of it all.

League Details

What to Expect

  • Competitive play with a welcoming culture — plus quarterly socials for community bonding.
  • 2-hour time blocks for each session, 8–10 weeks of play.
  • Assess your skill level here to see where you align in Elevate Volleyball events.

Registration & Info

  • Registration is done via Facebook Messenger, you can message either Elevate Volleyball or Alex Sum
  • Payment is done via venmo through: @elevatevolleyball
  • Captains deposit is due at registration with league fees due two weeks to new season starting
